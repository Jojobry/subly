## Detailed description

Stop paying for subscriptions you forgot about — without handing your financial data to yet another service.

Subly is a clean, free subscription tracker that lives in your browser and keeps everything **100% on your own device**. No account. No servers. No bank connections. No tracking. Just one tidy dashboard of what you actually pay.

⭐ WHY SUBLY

Most trackers ask you to sign up, link accounts, or trust their cloud. Subly doesn't. It has no backend at all — your subscriptions are stored locally and never leave your computer. It even works fully offline.

📊 EVERYTHING AT A GLANCE
• Total monthly and yearly spend, calculated instantly
• Active, free-trial, and due-soon counts
• Subscriptions grouped by status and sorted by next renewal

➕ FAST TO ADD
• Name, price, currency, billing cycle, renewal date, category, notes
• Weekly, monthly, quarterly, or yearly cycles
• Search across everything you track

🔔 NEVER GET SURPRISED
• On-device reminders before a renewal or free trial ends
• Choose how many days' notice you want
• No email required — notifications are generated locally and privately

🌍 BUILT FOR THE REAL WORLD
• 30+ currencies, combined into one total with built-in offline rates
• Dark, light, or system theme
• Past-due dates roll forward automatically to the next cycle

💾 YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
• Export everything to a JSON file, import it back anytime
• Uninstalling removes all stored data

🔒 PRIVACY-FIRST, GENUINELY
• No accounts, no logins
• No analytics, no telemetry, no ads
• No network requests of any kind
• Nothing collected means nothing to sell or leak
